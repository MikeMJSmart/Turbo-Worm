TURBO WORM - IPHONE HOME SCREEN BUILD

This is a Progressive Web App (PWA). It must be uploaded to an HTTPS website before iPhone installation.

IPHONE SETUP
1. Open the hosted game address in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button.
3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
4. Tap Add.
5. Open Turbo Worm from the new Home Screen icon.
6. Turn the iPhone sideways and tap POWER ON.

The service worker caches the game after the first successful load so it can reopen with limited or no internet.
